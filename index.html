Marwadi University

Vision

To be a leading institution that provides transformative education and fosters innovation, research, and leadership for the betterment of society.

Mission

  • To offer high-quality education and foster a culture of excellence.
  • To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values.
  • To promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • To contribute to sustainable and inclusive development.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  1. Apply fundamental knowledge of engineering and computing to solve real-world problems.
  2. Design and implement systems that meet specified needs using modern tools.
  3. Demonstrate leadership, ethics, and communication skills in professional settings.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech in Computer Engineering
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
  • B.Sc, BBA, B.Com, MBA, MCA
  • Ph.D. in various Fields
